What Is BusTracker

Bus Tracker is an application that uses GPS tracking system to show the real time position of public buses. Since travelling in public buses can be quite a hassle this app would provide an easier option to get information about the local buses travelling near the user’s location.



How It Works?

It would first track the user’s location and then show the real time position of all the public transportation vehicles and routes around that person. The user would get an option to select which yatayat they would like to track and the app would focus on that specific bus routes and show the position of the vehicles along with their estimated time of arrival.
